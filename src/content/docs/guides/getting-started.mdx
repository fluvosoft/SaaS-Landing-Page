## Setup Instructions

<Steps>

1. Choose Your Template Icon Select Your Lumiro Template
   Browse our collection of landing page templates and choose the one that best fits your business needs and style preferences.

2. Customize Your Content Icon Customize Content and Branding
   Update text, images, colors, and fonts to match your brand identity. Our templates make customization simple and intuitive.

3. Deploy Your Site Icon Deploy and Launch
   Follow our deployment guide to get your landing page live. We support various hosting platforms for easy setup.

</Steps>


## Best Practices

<Aside type="tip">
  Start with the default template design, then gradually customize elements to match your brand. This approach helps maintain design consistency.
</Aside>

For the best results, ensure your content is clear and concise, use high-quality images, and test your landing page across different devices before launching. Regular updates keep your page fresh and engaging.
