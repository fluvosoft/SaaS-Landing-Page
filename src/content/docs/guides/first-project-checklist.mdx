Building your first landing page with Lumiro can be exciting! This checklist will help ensure you have everything prepared and guide you through the process step by step. Follow this structured approach to create a professional landing page that converts visitors into customers.

## Pre-Launch Preparation

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Content Planning">
    - Prepare your business messaging, headlines, and call-to-action text
    - Gather high-quality images and graphics that represent your brand
    - Write compelling copy for all sections of your landing page
  </Card>
  <Card title="Brand Assets">
    - Have your logo ready in multiple formats (SVG, PNG)
    - Prepare brand colors and fonts to match your identity
    - Collect testimonials, case studies, or social proof if available
  </Card>
  <Card title="Technical Setup">
    - Choose a hosting platform for your landing page
    - Set up your domain name if needed
    - Prepare contact forms and integration requirements
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Building Your Landing Page
### Initial Setup
<Steps>
1. Select and download your preferred Lumiro template

2. Customize the template with your content, branding, and images

3. Test your landing page across different devices and browsers before launching
</Steps>
